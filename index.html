Smoked Pulled Ham

Foodista: Peas stew

About:

A hearty, nutritious stew simmered in a tomato sauce is a perfect choice for chilly days.

Ingredients:

  • 30 ml(2 tbs) olive oil
  • 300 g smoked cured ham, cut into cubes
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 400 g of fresh or frozen peas
  • 500 f tomato puree
  • 450 ml water
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • (or 1/2 tbs Vegeta Natur )
  • 400 g potatoes, peeled, and cut into small cubes
  • 1 tbs sugar

Preparation:

  1. Fry the cubed smoked cured ham in olive oil for about 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Stir in onion and saut√© for about 2 minutes.
  2. Add peas, tomato puree, water, salt and pepper or Vegeta Natur, stir to combine the ingrdients.
  3. Add potato cubes, and a spoonful of sugar at the end. Cover the pot, let the stew boil, then reduce the flame and cook for about 15 minutes or until the potatoes are soft.
